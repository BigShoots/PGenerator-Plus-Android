Test Patterns

Patch Size Applies to grayscale, color & saturation patches
Diagnostic
Visual setup patterns for display calibration. Use these to verify your display's basic picture settings before running a full calibration.
Grayscale (10-Point)
Neutral gray patches from 0% (black) to 100% (white) in 10% steps. Use with a meter to measure and correct grayscale tracking / white balance (RGB gains & offsets).
Grayscale (20-Point)
Finer 5% step grayscale patches for 20-point white balance calibration. Provides more detail in shadows and highlights than the 10-point set.
Color Checker
Primary (RGB) and secondary (CMY) color patches at 75% and 100% stimulus. Use with a colorimeter to verify and adjust the display's Color Management System (CMS) hue, saturation, and luminance per color.
75% Stimulus
100% Stimulus
Saturation Sweeps
Each color at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% saturation. Used to measure saturation tracking and fine-tune CMS saturation controls across the full range for each primary and secondary color.
